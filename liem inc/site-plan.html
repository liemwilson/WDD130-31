Overview

Purpose

For my website, I plan on showcasing some of my skills in developing a basic webpage. I will have an about me page, a page describing where people can contact me, links to my social accounts, and a page that will lead to some of my other work. In addition, I will include a page showing my process of design. I hope to make this website stand out in the way that I build it, the colors and fonts that I use, and the user friendliness of it as well. I plan on making this website to reach possible employers.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is future employers, people interested in web design, and people that would like to take advantage of the services that I offer.
